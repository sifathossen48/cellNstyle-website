Frequently Asked Questions

To become a franchisee, you will need to complete an application form, participate in an interview, and review our franchise agreement. Once approved, you will undergo comprehensive training and preparation before your store opens.

As a franchise partner, you will receive extensive support, including initial training, marketing assistance, operational guidance, and access to our network of suppliers to help you succeed.

The initial investment costs vary based on location and setup requirements. A detailed breakdown of costs will be provided during the application process.

While prior experience in the tech or retail industry is beneficial, it is not a strict requirement. We provide comprehensive training to help you learn the necessary skills to operate successfully.

Ongoing fees typically include a royalty percentage based on your sales and contributions to a national marketing fund. Specific details will be outlined in the franchise agreement.

We evaluate applications based on market potential and existing locations. If your area meets our criteria and there's no conflict with current franchises, you may be approved.

Franchisees will be trained to repair a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, PCs, and gaming consoles, using our established methods and techniques.

The timeline for opening a franchise varies, but once the agreement is signed, you can expect to be operational within a few months, depending on location setup and training completion.

Return on investment can vary based on several factors, including location, market demand, and your operational efficiency. We can provide estimated projections during the application process.

For more information, please reach out to us through our contact form or call our franchise development team at 940-380-9094.
